A Blast in Brazil!

Cheers to being young and traveling

About the Event

Brazil is a beautiful country which offers a plethora of exciting and unique activities. All of it's offerings make Brazil the ultimate trip destination for six college students, specifically, Rio de Janeiro. Brazil Trip 2025 will be an exciting and majestic adventure for the friend group AKA squad.

What to Expect

Let's Have the Time of Our Lives - Pitbull

  • New experiences: We will climb lanmarks we have never seen before, and go para gliding in the beautiful coasts of Brazil.
  • Experience the culture: We will visit local small businesses specializing in Braziolian foods and Brazilian goods.
  • Explore the nature: We will take a guided boat tour through the Amazon forest and learn about it's biodiversity.

Event Schedule

Exploring Brazil

In a casual setting, the group will walk around the city center in Rio. There will be an opportunity to visit stores, restaurants, and more.

Christ the Redeemer

The group will take a guided tour to the iconic Art Deco statue of Jesus located in Rio.

Beach

A tried and true squad activity, BEACH! Obvi, we will tan by the beautiful beaches of Brazil, perhaps it'll even be not that windy and we won't have to worry about any beach balls flying away :).
